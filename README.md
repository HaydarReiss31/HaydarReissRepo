# 🚀 CyberKit MiniBrowser

CyberKit is a high-performance, fast, and minimalist mini-browser engineered specifically for jailbroken iOS devices. It serves as an ultra-lightweight alternative to traditional, resource-heavy WebKit stock browsers, providing near-instant engine response and low memory overhead.

## ⚙️ Key Features & Technical Advantages

* **The WebKit Alternative:** Engineered to bypass the bloated overhead of stock iOS WebKit instances, allowing extremely fast loading times for quick queries and light surfing.
* **Minimalist UI:** Designed with a clean, distraction-free environment that maximizes screen real estate.
* **Resource Efficient:** Consumes minimal RAM and CPU cycles, ensuring your device stays cool and battery life is preserved.
* **Native Feel:** Built to seamlessly blend into your jailbroken SpringBoard environment without unnecessary clutter.
* **Compatibility:** Optimally tested and supported on iOS 14.0 and above.
